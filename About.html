About Me

I am Shivam Mishra, a computer science student with a passion for technology and creativity. My academic journey is fueled by a deep curiosity for coding, problem-solving, and exploring the limitless possibilities of software development. I thrive in the structured world of algorithms and data structures, constantly pushing myself to learn, innovate, and refine my technical skills. Beyond my studies, I find my artistic expression in street photography. With my camera in hand, I step into the bustling streets, capturing raw emotions, fleeting moments, and the beauty hidden in everyday life. Each frame I shoot tells a unique story—whether it's the energy of a crowded marketplace, the solitude of a quiet alley, or the candid expressions of people lost in their thoughts. Photography, to me, is more than just an art form; it's a way to preserve narratives that might otherwise be forgotten. I love the way technology and creativity intersect, and I am always looking for new ways to merge these two worlds. Whether I’m writing efficient code or composing the perfect shot, my goal is to create, innovate, and express my vision. I aspire to explore new frontiers where digital solutions meet artistic storytelling, bringing fresh perspectives to both fields.
